About the Artist

Sacipriya, also known as Ai Li, is a contemporary artist whose work explores the intersection of spirituality, emotion, and nature. Her paintings are deeply inspired by devotional themes, inner reflection, and the subtle beauty of the natural world. Working primarily with oil paints, she creates compositions that evoke stillness, movement, and transcendence. Each piece carries a meditative quality, imbued with tension, inviting the viewer into a space of contemplation and resonance through a balance of opposition and compromise. Her artistic journey reflects a synthesis of Eastern spiritual philosophy and expressive visual language, resulting in works that are both intimately introspective and expansively conceived.
